FirePool

     

Build your own forest. Expand your territory!
You start alone, in the midst of darkness.
Place lamps
to nourish your trees.

FirePool is a real-time strategy game taking place in a vast cavern. Your goal is to turn this dark underworld into a blooming garden of trees. But beware! Deadly flames from hell will come and swallow you.
