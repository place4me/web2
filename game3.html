HuntLand

Use tactics to hunt down your enemies.

There are multiple types of enemies, equipped with their own unique weapons.

You must deal with various calamities, such as fire, explosions, and more!

Combat can ensue anywhere. Chase or be chased!

You are an unwelcome visitor in a secret base inhabited by robotic creatures. They won't hurt you at first, but once you try to rob them they will attack you back (like in GTA). Destroy as many of them as possible, until you acquire enough money to let you escape this prison.
