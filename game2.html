Police Chase

Police cars are chasing you. Avoid them!

Try not to be surrounded by cops. Maneuver carefully using arrow keys.

Use items to either stun or destroy them.

Collect treasures while avoiding cops. Use various tactics to get yourself out of trouble!

You are a lost driver amidst a forest, constantly being pursued by police cars. Your job, as a professional thief, is to steal crumbs of gold that are hidden all over the place, while managing to run away from those who are desperate to arrest you. Good luck, treasure hunter!
